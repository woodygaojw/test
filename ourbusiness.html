LED Touch Guidance System: The soccer is equipped with multiple small LED lights that indicate specific touch area, helping users accurately perform training skills.


The automatic return system: The automatic Return System combines convenience and innovation, allowing soccer to automatically roll back to the user. By connecting to a mobile app, users can set a distance limit for the ball. When the app detects that the ball exceeds this limit, it activates the built-in drive technology to ensure the ball returns, eliminating the hassle of frequent retrieval and greatly enhancing training efficiency.

 

Sensors: The soccer is equipped with built-in sensors that can record the user's touch area and kick force, helping users better learn and master skills.
