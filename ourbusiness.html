We offer soccer fields equipped with high-tech camera systems. The cameras are strategically placed at various angles to fully capture the user's training moments. At the same time, the field is connected to our software system in real-time, providing users with instant feedback to help improve their performance.


Users can compete with others on our platform, earning points through matches to increase their rank (C, B, A, S levels, 50 points are required to level up, with 3 points awarded for a win and no points for a loss). Users who reach the S rank will have their matches live-streamed on the platform, adding a sense of competitive fun and honor.

 

Even if users cannot visit our football field, they can record training videos and upload them to the app. AI technology and professional coaches will provide detailed feedback, ensuring users can effectively improve anytime and anywhere.
